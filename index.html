A field guide from UCSB PaCE

Turn your data into reports worth reading.

This started at UCSB Professional & Continuing Education with course evaluations: thousands of raw survey rows that had to become per-instructor reports. We did not hand it to an AI and hope. We applied continuous-improvement principles, revised the process until it was solid, wrote scripts for the repeatable work (often with AI drafting them), and brought a model in for the one thing a script cannot do, turning free-text comments into a fair, cited summary. A person approved what shipped, and run on protected records, nothing ever left UCSB. The same process fits almost any data you have, open or sensitive, whether or not you write code.
